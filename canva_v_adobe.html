Your 1-Hour Workshop Playbook

0-15 mins: Canva's Opening Statement

Present Canva as the ultimate democratizer. Highlight: Ease of use, the "template king" status, and the incredible value of the all-in-one Canva for Education package. Show, don't just tell‚Äîdo a quick 2-minute demo of creating a worksheet from a template.

15-30 mins: Adobe Express's Opening Statement

Frame Adobe Express as the professional's choice. Highlight: Creative Cloud integration, "commercially-safe" Firefly AI, superior brand control features, and the career pathway it offers. Demo the AI-powered background removal or text-to-image feature.

30-50 mins: Rebuttal & Discussion

Time for the friendly clash! Use the pre-planned counter-arguments. Canva's Rebuttal: "Adobe's ecosystem is complex and expensive; Canva is powerful and independent." Adobe's Rebuttal: "Canva's templates can look generic; Adobe provides tools for true brand uniqueness and IP-safe AI."

50-60 mins: Audience Q&A & Wrap-Up

Open the floor for questions. Conclude by reiterating that the "best" tool is the one that best fits the specific classroom, project, or student need. Encourage everyone to try the free education versions of both.
